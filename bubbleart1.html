OSCAR & LILETTE


New!! “Soap Bubbles Art”



PERFECT TECHNIQUE

Charm and Creativity are the powers of the show where Oscar & Lilette bring the audience a dimension of utter fantasy. Their aim is to add a “Soap Bubbles Art Show” in their repertoire such as artists of many other various shows and they are proud to perform this Art and bring Unforgettable Emotions to the audience.


Introduction:

“Soap Bubbles Art”

With a background music, we present a carousel of special bubbles and various shapes such as: Bubble Cubic - Rectangle - Bubbles inside another Bubbles - Smoking Diamond - Carousel - Bouncing – Square - Vulcan – this kind of bubbles make a spectacular effect -we literally invite parents and children in a bubble. At the end of the show we propose an original Parade of Giants Bubbles.

Requirements

STAGING: stage area of 12 X14 ft (3x4m) 26” (3m) height will be the best black backdrop across the stage. Anyway the show can be presented in all situations.
STAGE CARPET: The Artist will provide a carpet to protect the floor from soap.
LIGHTING: Best lighting option moving lights. Best colors: light blue, pink, white, magenta, green. One follow spot (with magenta and pale blue jell)
SOUND: CD or MD player with audio mixer - in and out.

The show is completely international – no speaking parts during it.

Duration: 25 minutes

Logistics: Indoor show

Recommended: All ages
