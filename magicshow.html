MAGIC SHOW-on stage

Description:

A traditional Magic show, with a background music, Magic and Illusions that will charm the public.

On the 10th of June 2007 at Monte Carlo during the sport meeting of the “12th Olympic Games of small European Countries” organized annually by the Principality of Monaco. At the Gala Show, we have shown a “Magic Show” and the “Hand Shadows Show” in the presence of Prince Albert II of Monaco and the other members of the authorities.

We have had a great success.

PS:
It’s quite difficult for us to show how we perform through our video promotion. The magic show in reality work best with a continuous contact with the audience. Therefore, the spectators are the important for us and it’s because of that we have such great success.

Indicate:Absolutely for all ages.
Duration:25 minutes
