“Soap Bubbles Art”



This kind of entertainment offers all the magic of soap bubbles in their most pure and simple form. A straw of Plexiglas, an electronic cigarette and a simple tube of soap bubble liquid will be used to create wonderful sculptures only a few inches away from the guests.
A performance of miniature Bubbles is suitable for gala Dinners, Cocktail Parties, Happy Hours Conventions and any other situation in close contact from people present.
The entertainment involves construction of small sculptures with soap bubbles and the performing style of “walk around” where the artist is performing directly to small group of people at a time. The audience can be standing at a cocktail party or having a break between meals during an elegant dinner.


Requirements

Recommended: For All Ages

Duration: minimum 30 minutes, also depending number of guests.

Logistics: Indoor show
