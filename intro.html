Oscar & Lilette
Show

Oscar & Marie Ilette Baraldi,are professional artists performing their show for Hotels, Events, Cruise Ships, Company Conventions, Casino’s, etc…
In the context for an important Event that requires a moment of entertainment that take place on stage, we can offer various possibilities to suit your Event.
Many different solutions are available for your needs:
• 5 – 6 minutes for TV appearances.
• 10 – 12 – 15 minutes for Variety Show, Company Conventions, and Events.
• 35 – 40 minutes for a complete Show suitable for all situations.
Practically you can choose the time necessary and you can “package” the show for the situation you need, remaining always in the limit (for technical reason).

For example:
Our performance last 45 minutes:
Magic Show    20’
Hand Shadows Show   5’
Balloons Carnival on stage   10’

For short embarking as “Inter port” ideal for Cruise.

Magic Close-up and Balloon Carnival
Entertainment can also be done before the show on stage, Oscar can entertain the guest with Magic Close-up and Lilette can also entertain with the Balloons Sculpturing, you can choose the time you need.
