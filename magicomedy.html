MAGIC COMEDY

Imagine a mocking stand-up comedian in the guise of a bizarre magician or a mad magician in the shoes of a sophisticated comedian, surreal. This is the Magic Oscar!

The irony, originality, and bars burst completely devoid of vulgarity, are the foundation of this humour, never banal, sophisticated yet popular, suitable for any audience, specialist in this kind of entertainment, Oscar is certainly considered one of the most complete for this kind of entertainment.
A mixture of comedy and magic tricks without even understanding where it ends where it begins, and the magician comedian interacting and evolving with the public are two further key of this show, absolutely valid for all ages.

N.B:The show is performing only in Italian language.
Duration: 45 minutes.
