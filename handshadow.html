HAND SHADOWS SHOW

Description:
On a screen a special flood light projects some wonderful animated caricatures, some pictures of African animals and pictures of well-known personalities such as Ray Charles, Liza Minnelli, the Pope Jean Paul II and others. A photograph of a politician of our time appears on the left side of the screen while on the right some hand shadows are executed. Big moment of emotion with Charlie Chaplin where the audience is really moved by a historical and unforgettable music. During the whole show of imagination, Light and Shadows, the public feels like they are dreaming with eyes wide opened. The particularity of this show is that 95% of its content is executed only with fingers(hands).



PS:
If you wish that the shadow of the profile of a person who you consider as a VIP, during an important meeting in a gala show, we can produce the shadow of his face. A photography of his face should be sent two weeks before the program. (The characteristic of the photo should be a ¾ profile.)

Indicate:For all ages
Duration:25 minutes
