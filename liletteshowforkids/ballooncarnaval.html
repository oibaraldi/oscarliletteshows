BALLOONS CARNIVAL


DO YOU STILL THINK THAT BALLOONS IS ONLY FOR KIDS?

Balloons symbolize cheerfulness, it was always present in funny parties during our childhood as balloons create colorful events.


Description

Just imagine a kid and one adult with a bizarre likeable hat or with the big ears of Mickey Mouse! the kid will look cute, while the adult will look funny and will keep himself amused.

Using special balloons, Lilette twist them in different forms like animals, flowers, strange hats, and character of Walt Disney, and many other shapes. The balloons are distributed to the kids during the show, and the kids can enjoy themselves in a funny carnival atmosphere.

The Balloons Carnival is an original avant-garde entertainment specially for situation where you can have a direct contact with the audiences, indicate for gala dinner, happy hours, cocktail, private party, carnival, Halloween, for 31 December of the San Silvestre night, etc.

Lilette evolve with discretion and elegance with the spectators thus creating a humorous situation, and the sculptures she twists are given to the participants. The show is ideal to be done on stage, the back-ground music create a funny atmosphere. A colorful and funny show to ensure a big success with the Balloons Carnival Show.

Focus point:
• Visual everywhere.
• Colorful and very Funny.
• People present in the audiences will enjoy themselves with photography of friends and their family at this event.

P.S
At the beginning of her career she was specialized in the arts of balloons sculpturing and in the way to improve and to perform a perfect quality show.
She participle regularly at the international congress of “Balloons Arts Convention International” of the (Championships World for the Balloons Sculpturing) who take place in Ottawa (Canada) and Lilette win the 4th place in the category of show performances.

Recommended:For all events.

Show on stage “Balloons Carnival”
Duration: 10 / 15 minutes.

Entertainment On client’s request:Either magic close up or show on stage.
