BIOGRAPHY

MARIE ILETTE VICTOIRE BARALDI

Welcome! My Name is Marie Ilette Victoire Baraldi, I was born in Mauritius, a paradise Island in the Indian Ocean known for his beautiful tropical beach and for the smile of the local people. I left Mauritius for Italy to continue my studies in a school in Rome.

During summer holidays, I got a job as animator for Kids in a touristic hotel village in Sardinia. I returned in Rome after four months, I was happy to see my friends and to continue my studies.

One day, my friends and I went to the famous circus “Holliday on Ice”, their performance of an Artist Clown make me stupefy, he performed a magic show for Kids with gigantic bubbles soap and very colorful balloons sculpture, the background music create a wonderful magic atmosphere, for me it seems like dreaming.

From this moment, my life changes. The day after, I try to look for and try to get some idea to begging these arts and learn from what I saw from this Artist. My goal was to stupefy kids when I do animation for them in summer holidays and in the Ludotech where I leaved. After I got a stage like operator touristic organized by the tour operator Valtur, at the same time I was a member of the Club Magico Italiano in Rome where I learn arts of Magic, Balloons Sculpturing and how to create gigantic “Soap Bubbles”.

After many experiences in the field of animation, touristic entertainment like polyvalent animator, like leader of the Kids Club, and also leader of a big staff of animation, I have had the opportunity to worked in some country in Europe like Spain (Tenerife), France (Cannes), Greece (Rode), and I also collaborated with some Cruises Company, I was very fortunate to visit continents like, North Africa, America Latina, Australia etc.

Then I finished my studies; I got my degree for the vocational training. I teach in the elementary government school in Rome. In summer 1987 I worked at Santa Margherita di Pula in Sardinia exactly at the Fort Hotel Village, during a dinner gala for a convention of the group Fiat, a professional artist named Oscar Baraldi performed a “Hand Shadows Show”. The show was spectacular with many emotional moments, and he got a big success by the audiences.

By this moment, someone and something change my life, actually Oscar Baraldi is now my husband. I left my job as teacher to share together our artistic life in the show business where I built a show special for kids; we propose different kind of Show for Cruises Ships, for greats prestigious Hotels, Conventions, Events, and Casino etc.In 1985 I won the 3rd place for the category of Magic show on stage at the International congress at Saint Vincent.

When we are not engaged to travelling around the world, as I’m a specialist to entertain Kids, I propose my show for school, Hotels, commercial centers, Birthday parties, etc.
