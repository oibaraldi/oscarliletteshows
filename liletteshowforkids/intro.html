INTRODUCTION

People believe that doing a show for children is relatively simple.

In reality, it is often the opposite, to succeed in attracting and keeping the attention of children is very challenging and fundamental which needs an artist experience, specially the way the show is presented.
We must succeed in creating an atmosphere of fun, mystery, charm and complicity, and in fact, it’s on these points that I base my presentation.
