MAGIC CLOSE UP

Description:

An avant-garde entertainment adapted for various situations such as Gala Dinner, Cocktails, Party, Happy hour, etc. The artist has a close contact with the audience. It is a series of magic tricks with full of emotion, technique and dexterity, using small objects such as cards, coins, dices, ropes etc. The Magic Close-up has a great contact to the public and creates a big illusion with small objects. The audience shares the opportunity to have a close contact with the magician and watch a perfect magic illusion. This way of presenting magical effects is different from seeing the magician just on the stage. This method of presenting magical effects as such a near distance guarantees a successful evening.


TELEVISION PROGRAM
The “Magic close-up” is adaptable in TV shows like entertainment or to teach magic tricks, when somebody wants to advertise in any of their commercial spot. The interested person can request the magician to package a specific magic trick adapted as he need to advertise his product.

Duration of the entertainment:On client request.
